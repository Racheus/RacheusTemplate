对于机械臂的拉格朗日动力学方程，可以写成如下形式：
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    \frac{d}{dt}(\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}_i}) - \frac{\partial L}{\partial q_i} &= Q_i
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}

Where Lagrangian $L$ is defined as:
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    L &= T - V
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}
